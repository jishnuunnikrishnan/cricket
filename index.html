CRICKET FRIENDS KALLIASSERY

ABOUT US

"Cricket friends kalliassery" is a well known cricket club in kannur district. The club was established on 21 january 2018 . The founders of the club are jiju vallithode and vishak vallithode. The home ground of the club is in vallithode where cricket practise matches and other tournaments takes place everyday. The club consists of 35 members and already many new talented players have emerged from here. The team participated in many tournamnets and have already won many of it.
